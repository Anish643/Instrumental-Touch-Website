What I Do

Meet Anish Peshwe, a virtuoso percussionist whose rhythmic journey spans an impressive 13 years. With a mastery extending across diverse percussion instruments like Tabla, Cajon, Djembe, Darbuka, and more, Anish's musical odyssey is a harmonious blend of tradition and innovation.

Who am I

Delving deep into the nuances of classical Djembe, Anish's commitment to continuous learning is as evident as his passion for rhythmic exploration. Boasting an extensive classical tabla training of 8 years, Anish seamlessly intertwines the traditional with the contemporary, venturing into the realm of Westernized instruments over the past 5 years.
